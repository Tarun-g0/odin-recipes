Maggie

Description

Maggie is a tasty indian dish just like noodles, there are various wasys to prepare maggie with veggies, egg and etc. but the recipe which is specified here is a simple tasty maggie.

Ingridients

  • maggie packet
  • water
  • masala packet inside the maggie packet

How to prepare

  • step 1

    • let the water boil to a certain temperature

  • step 2

    • put the maggie in the boiling water

  • Step 3

    • add the masala to the maggie after it softens and little and mix it.
